üéØ Problem Statement

The Challenge

Finding optimal hyperparameters is challenging due to:

  • Large search spaces with many parameters
  • Expensive evaluations requiring full model training
  • Non-convex landscapes with local optima

Our Objective

Compare three optimization strategies:

  • Grid Search
  • Random Search
  • Bayesian Optimization (Optuna)

üî¨ Optimization Methods

üìä Grid Search

Exhaustive

  • Evaluates all combinations
  • Guaranteed coverage
  • Slow for large spaces

üé≤ Random Search

Stochastic

  • Random sampling
  • Fast execution
  • Good exploration

üß† Bayesian (Optuna)

Model-based

  • Learns from history
  • Intelligent sampling
  • Most efficient
